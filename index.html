Peptides in Research: Potential Benefits

Peptides are short chains of amino acids that serve as signaling molecules in biological systems. Research explores their roles in various pathways.

  • Tissue Repair & Recovery: Studies on compounds like BPC-157 and TB-500 for wound healing models.
  • Metabolic Health: Research into fat metabolism, energy (e.g., MOTS-c, AOD9604).
  • Anti-Aging & Cellular Health: Telomere, collagen, and longevity pathway investigations (Epithalon, GHK-Cu).
  • Endocrine & Growth: GH secretagogues like CJC-1295/Ipamorelin in hormone signaling studies.
